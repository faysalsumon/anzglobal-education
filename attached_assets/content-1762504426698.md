#### ABOUT COURSE

The **Advanced Diploma of Hospitality Management** at Hilton Academy is designed for individuals looking to advance their careers in the hospitality industry. Based in Melbourne, this course covers essential topics such as business operations, customer service, human resource management, marketing, and financial management. Students will gain practical skills in leadership and management, preparing them for senior roles in the hospitality sector. Graduates will be equipped to work in high-level management positions across a range of hospitality settings, from hotels to event management.

The **Advanced Diploma of Hospitality Management** at Hilton Academy is a comprehensive program tailored for those seeking to develop advanced skills in managing hospitality businesses. Offered in Melbourne, this course provides students with the knowledge and expertise to take on senior leadership roles within the dynamic hospitality industry.

The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, including leadership, financial management, human resources, marketing, and customer service. Students will learn how to manage operations in hospitality organizations, ensure quality service, develop and manage teams, and create effective marketing strategies. The program also includes practical learning experiences, allowing students to apply their skills in real-world scenarios.

A key feature of the **Advanced Diploma of Hospitality Management** is its focus on industry-relevant skills. Students will have access to state-of-the-art facilities, including a commercial kitchen, where they can refine their skills in a hands-on setting. The course combines classroom learning with practical training, preparing students for the fast-paced, customer-focused environment of the hospitality sector.

Hilton Academy’s experienced faculty brings a wealth of industry knowledge and expertise to the classroom. Students will benefit from personalized mentoring and support, helping them develop the leadership qualities needed to manage hospitality teams and operations effectively.

Upon completion of the **Advanced Diploma of Hospitality Management**, graduates will be ready to pursue senior roles in the hospitality industry, including positions such as hotel manager, restaurant manager, event coordinator, or food and beverage director. The qualification also provides a pathway to further studies in management or related fields.
